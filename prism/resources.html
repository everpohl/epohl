A draft resource hub with room to grow.

This page is being built as part of Prism’s launch-ready site structure. For now, the sections below use placeholder copy so the layout, navigation, and categories are ready before final resource details are approved.

The goal is simple: make it easy for students to find a first step, whether they are looking for urgent support, campus services, community care, or guidance on where to start.

Page Plan

Draft structure for the launch version.

This page is organized so it can launch in a semi-complete state without feeling empty. Each section can hold a few approved links at first and grow later as Prism adds more campus and community recommendations.

Start with categories

Group resources by the kind of help someone is looking for, not by internal planning language or office names alone.

Keep first steps visible

Every section should help visitors answer one question quickly: where do I go next if I need help, information, or connection?

Use placeholders on purpose

Temporary descriptions can stay simple and polished until final links, names, and wording are approved by the e-board.
