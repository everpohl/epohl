Why this structure is better

The root homepage should represent you, while Prism should live in its own folder so its URL becomes a clear subsection: `webpages.charlotte.edu/epohl/prism/`.

Good web habit

Keeping separate projects in separate directories makes navigation, hosting, and future maintenance much easier. It is the same basic idea as separating routes in a larger app.
