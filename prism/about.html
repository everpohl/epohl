About Prism

A student organization centered on belonging and action.

Prism exists to help LGBTQ+ students and allies find community, share support resources, and take part in meaningful advocacy at UNC Charlotte.

What Prism focuses on

Building a welcoming campus community, creating visible support for students, and helping members take part in conversations and campaigns that matter.

What leadership looks like

Student leaders help host events, answer questions, connect members to resources, and keep Prism’s advocacy and community work moving from idea to action.
