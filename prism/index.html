About Prism

More than a club, Prism is a support network.

A strong student organization homepage should explain its purpose quickly. This section gives visitors an immediate sense of what Prism offers and why it matters.

Build Community

Create spaces where students can meet one another, feel welcomed, and make meaningful connections on campus.

Share Resources

Point students toward practical support, whether they need campus information, affirming services, or guidance on where to begin.

Support Advocacy

Organize around issues that affect LGBTQ+ students and help people participate in campaigns, letters, and collective action.
